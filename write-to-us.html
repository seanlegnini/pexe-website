Write to Us

The PEXE Lab is two people and an open question — actually, two open questions. If they're your questions too, we'd like to hear from you.

There's no membership or application process, we have an email address and a genuine interest in conversation. If our work resonates with you, please reach out.

For the podcast Join us for a podcast episode Come talk to us about your line with AI or share your scholarship as a public pedagogue. Join us in public conversation. For an event Invite us to speak with you We love speaking on phenomenology and education, experiential learning reclaimed, and the nuanced balance of humanity and artificial intelligence in teaching and learning. Tell us when and where and we'd love to join you! For everything else Write to us if our questions are your questions If you're a scholar, practitioner, researcher, anyone who shares our thoughts, we'd love to hear what you're doing and where you're doing it.
Or simply write: contact@pexelab.org
