The Phenomenology & Experiential Education Lab

What is it to teach? What is it to learn?

PEXE Lab is a working space for those two questions. "Experiential" is a word worth reclaiming. It's been thinned to a shorthand for field trips and hands-on activities; we think experience itself deserves more careful attention than that, and recovering what it can mean is part of the work. That recovery is phenomenological work — slow, careful, grounded in lived experience — and we're doing it in public, through essays, a podcast, and ongoing research. Come think alongside us.
