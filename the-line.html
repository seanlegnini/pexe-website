The Line

A podcast from the PEXE Lab

Everyone has a line they won't let AI cross — a task, a relationship, a piece of work that has to stay human. But where exactly is that line, and why? Each episode, Sean Legnini and Matthew Kruger-Ross sit down with someone from a different field and ask: where's your line? Part interview, part investigation into the creation of a social ethics of AI in real time.
